Digital Marketing Intern N&WA

We are looking for a Digital Marketing Intern for a position located in the Casablanca office and reporting to Cluster Digital Marketing Manager.

If you are dynamic, open-minded, curious and creative, you will be a great addition to our team for this 6-month paid internship.

What You Will Be Doing

As a Digital Marketing Intern at JTI N&WA, you will

Monitor website uptime, performance, and user experience using tools like Google Analytics, UptimeRobot, or similar.
Assist in deploying updates, patches, and content changes across websites and digital platforms.
Support the QA process by testing website functionality across browsers and devices.
Help identify and troubleshoot website issues, bugs, or downtime incidents.
Collaborate with developers and designers to ensure seamless digital operations.
Maintain documentation of website configurations, updates, and monitoring logs.
Assist in implementing SEO best practices and performance optimization strategies.
Contribute to the development of dashboards and reports on website health and KPIs.

Requirements

Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Digital marketing , Digital Media, or a related field.
Familiarity with website CMS platforms (e.g., WordPress, Joomla, Drupal).
Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and web hosting environments.
Experience with website monitoring tools is a plus.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks

Preferred Skills

Exposure to web analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Hotjar).
Understanding of SEO, page speed optimization, and accessibility standards.
Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git).
